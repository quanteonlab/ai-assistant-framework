The stakeholder view in CSR emphasizes a corporation's duty to consider not only shareholders but also other groups affected by corporate decisions, such as employees, customers, creditors, suppliers, and the community. Advocacy groups like environmental or animal rights organizations may also be stakeholders.
:p Who are considered stakeholders in CSR?
??x
