#### Quiet Enjoyment Covenant
The covenant of quiet enjoyment in a lease contract means that neither the landlord nor anyone else with superior title to the property will disturb the tenant’s use and enjoyment during the lease term. If breached, tenants can terminate the lease and seek damages.

??x
Quiet enjoyment in a lease ensures that tenants are not disturbed by the landlord or others who have more significant rights over the property. Breach of this covenant allows tenants to end their lease and sue for compensation if their use and enjoyment of the property is disrupted.
