#### Outer Space Treaty (1967)
Background context: The Outer Space Treaty is a foundational document in international space law, establishing the framework for subsequent treaties. It declares outer space to be free for exploration and use by all nations and prohibits certain activities like weaponization.
:p What are the key provisions of the Outer Space Treaty?
??x
Key provisions include:
- Outer space is free for exploration and use by all nations (Article I).
- Celestial bodies, including the moon, planets, asteroids, etc., cannot be appropriated by any single nation (Article II).
- Space objects must only be used for peaceful purposes and no weapons of mass destruction are allowed in outer space (Article IV).
- Each state is responsible for its activities in outer space, both government and private entities require authorization and supervision by a government (Article VI).
- Each state retains jurisdiction and control over its space objects and personnel on them (Article VIII).
- States must be liable for damage caused by their space objects (Article VII).
- Space exploration should avoid harmful contamination (Article IX).
