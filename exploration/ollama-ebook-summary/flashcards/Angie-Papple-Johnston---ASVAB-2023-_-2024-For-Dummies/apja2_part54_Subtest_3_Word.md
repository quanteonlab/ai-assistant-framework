:p Convert 24% to a fraction.
??x

\[ 24\% = \frac{24}{100} \]

Then simplify the fraction by dividing both the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD), which is 4:

\[ \frac{24}{100} = \frac{24 \div 4}{100 \div 4} = \frac{6}{25} \]
