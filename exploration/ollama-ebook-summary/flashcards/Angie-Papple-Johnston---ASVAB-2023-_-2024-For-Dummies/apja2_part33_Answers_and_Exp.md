#### Mantle Composition and Structure
Explanation of the mantle, which accounts for about two-thirds of Earth’s mass, divided into different layers based on their composition and behavior during earthquakes.

:p What are the three main layers within the mantle, and what distinguishes them?
??x
The three main layers within the mantle are:
- **Mesosphere**: Located in the lower mantle, surrounding Earth's outer core. Temperatures are high enough to melt rock but pressure keeps materials solid.
- **Asthenosphere**: In the middle part of the mantle. Rocks there move like glaciers due to plastic flow and temperatures still cause some melting.
- **Lithosphere**: The uppermost part of the mantle attached to the crust, composed of solid, thick, and brittle rocks.
