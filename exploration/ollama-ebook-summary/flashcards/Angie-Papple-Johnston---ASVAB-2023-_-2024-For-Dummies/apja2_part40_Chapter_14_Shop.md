#### Levels (Torpedo Level)
Background context: Levels show whether a horizontal surface is true. Torpedo levels have small tubes filled with liquid and an air bubble that indicates when the surface is level.

:p How does a torpedo level work to determine if a surface is level?
??x
A torpedo level works by placing it on a surface, allowing the air bubble to settle in the center of the tube. If the bubble remains centered, the surface is level.
x??
