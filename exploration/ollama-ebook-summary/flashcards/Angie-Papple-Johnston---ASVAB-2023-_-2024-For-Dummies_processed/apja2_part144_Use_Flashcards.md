#### Volume Formulas for 3-D Solids
The ASVAB includes questions on calculating volumes of various 3-D solids. Familiarity with these formulas is essential.

:p What are the volume formulas for a rectangular solid and a cylinder?
??x

For a rectangular solid (box), the formula to find the volume $V$ is:

$$V = lwh$$where $ l $is length,$ w $ is width, and $ h$ is height.

For a cylinder, the formula is:
$$V = \pi r^2 h$$

Here,$\pi $(pi) is approximately 3.14159,$ r $ is the radius of the circular base, and $ h$ is the height of the cylinder.
